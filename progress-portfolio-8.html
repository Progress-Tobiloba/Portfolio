I'm a web designer based in Nigeria with 3 years of hands-on experience building websites that look great and feel right. I work across Figma, WordPress, and Wix — from initial concept all the way to a live, polished site.

My process starts in Figma where I design every detail before touching a builder. That means fewer revisions, cleaner results, and websites clients actually love. I'm also a Computer Engineering student at Obafemi Awolowo University, which means I understand the technical side of what makes a site fast and reliable.

Outside design, I run @techwithprogress — a tech education channel on Instagram and TikTok where I teach web design and development concepts to beginners.
